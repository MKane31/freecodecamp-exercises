Lemon Drizzle Muffins

Bright, zesty, and irresistibly moist, these lemon drizzle muffins are the perfect pick-me-up for any time of day. Easy to whip up and bursting with citrus flavor, they make a refreshing treat that’s light but satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the melted butter, milk, egg, lemon zest, and lemon juice.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and stir just until combined.
  5. Spoon the batter into the muffin liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  6. Bake for 15–18 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool slightly before serving!
